Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Smart Prompt Solutions, our features, pricing, and how to get the most out of our AI prompts.

How do I get started with Smart Prompt Solutions? +

Getting started is easy! Sign up for our free trial to get access to 5 professional prompts immediately. No credit card required. You'll receive an email with your sample pack and access instructions within minutes of signing up.

What AI tools do your prompts work with? +

Our prompts work with all major AI platforms including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini (formerly Bard), Bing Chat, and any other text-based AI tool. They're designed to be platform-agnostic and work across different AI models.

Can I change plans anytime? +

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing, while downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

Is there a money-back guarantee? +

Absolutely! We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with our prompts for any reason, contact us within 14 days for a full refund—no questions asked.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through encrypted payment systems, and we never store your payment information.

How do custom prompt credits work? +

Premium users receive 5 custom prompt credits monthly. Submit a request describing your specific needs, industry, or use case, and our team of prompt engineers will create personalized prompts within 3-5 business days. Unused credits don't roll over.

Can I use prompts for client work? +

Yes! All our prompts can be used for client work, including agencies, consultants, and freelancers. Premium plans include white-label options for professional client presentations. You retain full commercial rights to content created using our prompts.

How often do you add new prompts? +

We release new prompts monthly, with Pro users receiving 1 featured pack and Premium users receiving 2 featured packs per month. We also continuously update existing prompts based on user feedback and AI model improvements.

Do you have an API for developers? +

Yes! Premium subscribers get access to our RESTful API for integrating prompts into their own applications. The API includes endpoints for retrieving prompts, tracking usage, and managing custom prompts. Full documentation is available in your dashboard.

Can I export my prompts and data? +

Yes! You can export all your prompts, custom categories, and usage data at any time. We support CSV, JSON, and PDF formats. This ensures you always have access to your data, even if you decide to cancel your subscription.

Is my data secure and private? +

Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade security with SSL encryption, secure data centers, and strict privacy controls. We never share your data with third parties, and you can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How much time can I expect to save? +

Our users typically save 10-15 hours per week on content creation, marketing tasks, and business communications. The exact time saved depends on your usage, but most see immediate improvements in productivity from day one.

What if I need help or have questions? +

We provide comprehensive support through multiple channels: email support for all users, priority support for Pro/Premium users, community forum, extensive documentation, and video tutorials. Premium users also get access to personal onboarding calls.
