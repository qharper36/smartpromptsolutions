How do I use AI prompts? ▼

Using our AI prompts is simple:

  1. Download your prompt pack from your dashboard
  2. Copy the prompt you want to use
  3. Paste it into your AI tool (ChatGPT, GPT-4, Claude, etc.)
  4. Fill in any variables (like [YOUR BUSINESS], [PRODUCT NAME])
  5. Hit enter and get your professional content!

Each prompt comes with detailed instructions and examples to help you get the best results.

Which AI platforms are supported? ▼

Our prompts work with any AI text generator, including:

  • • ChatGPT (3.5 and 4)
  • • GPT-4 API
  • • Claude (Anthropic)
  • • Jasper AI

  • • Any OpenAI-powered tool

The prompts are platform-agnostic and designed to work with any modern AI writing tool.
